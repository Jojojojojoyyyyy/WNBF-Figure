Part 3 · Nutrition

Fuel to build

Protein stays at your current ~140 g — the added calories come from carbs, on top. That's the lever for more muscle.

Gout / uric-acid: lean on the low-purine backbone — whey, egg whites, dairy, whole egg. Treat tofu/tempeh/legumes as rotation (spread across the day) and mushrooms as a vegetable. Hydrate, but ~3.5–4.5 L is plenty.
